SAUDI DATES


Khalas


20 Min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Khalas Maknooz dates
  • 1 cup roasted and ground oats
  • 2/1 cup peanut butter or almond
  • 1 tbsp shredded coconut
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Preparation

In saucepan, combine all
ingredients together and mix
them in the electric mixer. Roll
them or shape them as you like.
Finally put it into the fridge.
